## 🌟 **What is Free Class?**

**Free Class** is the ultimate travel optimization category in Lobsters Bonvoyå, designed for travelers who want to achieve maximum value with zero cost. It represents the pinnacle of financial freedom travel, where every journey becomes an investment in skills, experiences, and community connections.

### **Core Philosophy:**
- **Zero Cost, Maximum Value**: Travel without spending money while gaining priceless experiences
